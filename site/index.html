FAQ

FAQ

FAQ

Is YourOS just another PKM system or Obsidian Vault? If so, how does it differ from the others?

No, YourOS presents an innovative approach to simplify personal knowledge management, track personal well-being, and handle finances. By centralizing these functions, users can access a holistic perspective of their lives and establish a tailored life management system. The flexible nature of YourOS enables easy integration, customization, and the choice to activate different features, including the ability for users to opt out of monitoring their well-being. In essence, it can be viewed as an extension of the Second Brain concept, combining journaling, task management, well-being tracking, and financial analysis into a single tool.

Do you store any data?

No, YourOS is designed to be a private system. The data is stored locally and on your personal Google Account.

Is any registration required?

No, YourOS only needs a Google Account and the Obsidian or Notion Apps.

How much does YourOS cost?

YourOS is offered for free.

How much time does it take to configure YourOS?

The YourOS Obsidian Vault comes pre-configured, while the Dashboards require a one-time configuration process. Comprehensive video and written documentation guides are provided, making it easy to follow. Users typically spend an average of 30-45 minutes configuring the system.

Are there any pre-requirements to be able to configure and use YourOS? Do I need to code?

Users are encouraged to possess a basic knowledge of Google Sheets and Obsidian (or Notion). No coding expertise is necessary. Since setting up the dashboards involves some configuration, users may need to feel comfortable following an implementation guide.

Is YourOS a plugin?

No, YourOS includes the Obsidian Vault, as well as Dashboards created using Google Looker and connected to Google Sheets/Google Forms. These dashboards can be embedded within Obsidian or Notion as an iframe.

I use Notion, how can I implement YourOS in my system?

For Notion users, I recommend utilizing YourOS Analytics. This system provides a tracker for managing well-being and finances by utilizing Google Looker, Google Sheets, and Google Forms, as well as embedding the dashboards through an iframe. It also comes with detailed video and written guides for comprehensive assistance.

Is there a support email?

Sure, for any questions users can contact: yourosconfiguration@gmail.com

The story behind YourOS

YourOS was born out of a real need I was experiencing two years ago.


As a tech enthusiast, I've tested countless note-taking apps and productivity systems. However, none of them provided a structured framework to help me reflect on and holistically manage my life. It became clear that these solutions were limited and inflexible, forcing users to conform to their pre-set structures.

This realization was not unique to me; many others were experiencing the same frustrations.


Having a decade of experience in the digital health industry, I combined my product management and data analytics skills to create YourOS, a robust and future-proof framework designed to help individuals manage their lives effectively.

Now, I'm thrilled to share YourOS with the world, as it has brought contentment and purpose to my life, I hope it will do the same to thousands of others.

Fun Fact: In the process of building YourOS I become a marathon runner :)
