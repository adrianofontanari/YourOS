# Introduction — the Promise of YourOS

## Introduction - the Promise of YourOS

This concise handbook covers the principles of how YourOS was built as well as its core components and configuration. The guide is complementary to the YourOS Essentials and YourOS Configuration videos.  

YourOS was born out of a real need I was experiencing two years ago.

In a world where apps come and go, we easily end up with dozens of solutions to manage our productivity, well-being, and finances; wasting precious time in understanding what is the right tool, lately knowing its limitations, and paying for several subscriptions that do not offer what we are looking for. After a year we are again searching for another app as the company was shut down or acquired or increased their subscription costs.

As a tech enthusiast, I've tested countless note-taking apps and productivity systems. However, none of them provided a structured framework to help me reflect on and holistically manage my life. It became clear that these solutions were limited and inflexible, forcing users to conform to their pre-set structures. This realization was not unique to me; many others were experiencing the same frustrations. 

Many productivity users start with the belief that there is the right way to do things and end up wasting a lot of time building up systems that clutter their lives. The honest answer is that there is no such system. The world constantly, changes, we change and so do our way of doing things.
As an example, many users who find out about obsidian are easily overwhelmed by its potential and get lost in thousands of tutorial videos on YouTube with complex and unnecessary customizations. And get even more overwhelmed.

Having a decade of experience in the digital health industry, I combined my product management and data analytics skills to create YourOS, a robust and future-proof framework designed to help individuals manage their lives effectively. 
YourOS is the result of two years of continuous research and practice during which I carefully tested and selected the best tools that followed these principles.

It follows the Japanese philosophy of Shurari: i. learn the foundations, ii. make innovation, iii. go your own way.

Before moving to step II. and iii., whereas many people start and fail, we will cover what are your needs needs and the functional requirements of a unified system. So that the system is flexible to achieve our goals.

Between buying a solution or building it, YourOS is in the middle. It will provide a packaged system with the theory to configure and edit it so that you will create your perfect system.

Now, I'm thrilled to share YourOS with the world, as it has brought contentment and purpose to my life, I hope it will do the same to thousands of others.

Fun Fact: In the process of building YourOS I become a marathon runner :)
