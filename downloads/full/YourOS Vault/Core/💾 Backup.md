# 💾 Backup

> [!tip] A formal backup strategy allows you to never miss a file. I would recommend backing up YourOS vault in its integrity at least once a month. Much can be said about backup strategies (e.g. see the 3-2-1 backup rule), the important thing is to have a regular copy of your data in several places. For privacy-focused storage, a Network-attached storage (NAS) could be an option. However, most users may just need an external HD. Use this note to formalize your backup strategy and backup info (e.g. date of last backup). Do not forget to put it into action by creating a recurring task). You may also want to explore more advanced cybersecurity practices, such as buying a physical 2FA security key (e.g. Yubico).

## Backup Logs:
Example:
- Last Backup: January 10, 2024
- Additional info: Saved on iCloud + offline HD. Photos not exported
