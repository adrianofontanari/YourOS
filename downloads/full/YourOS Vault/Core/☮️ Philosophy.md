# Personal Life Manifesto
- A personal manifesto is a written statement outlining your aspirations and desires in life. This is also important for job search. Online there are many guides on how to write a life manifesto. In this section, you could also write down your life principles. Would you like to define your life principles and you do not know where to start? Ray Dalio's books can be a good starting point. These can be later added to the [[Quotebook]] and appear in the daily journal.
